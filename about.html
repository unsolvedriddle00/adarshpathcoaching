Our Story

Adarsh Path Coaching Classes was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide quality education that goes beyond textbooks and examinations. Established over a decade ago, we have grown from a small tutoring center to one of the most trusted names in CBSE coaching for classes 1st to 10th.

Our journey has been marked by countless success stories, dedicated educators, and satisfied parents who have entrusted us with their children's academic future. We believe that every student is unique and deserves individual attention to unlock their full potential.
